## Idea 1: "Casino Noir" — Art Deco Meets Digital

**Design Movement:** Art Deco Revival with Film Noir undertones — 1920s Monte Carlo reinterpreted through a modern digital lens.

**Core Principles:**
1. Geometric precision — sharp angles, gold trim lines, symmetrical compositions
2. Dramatic contrast — deep blacks against warm golds and crisp whites
3. Tactile luxury — surfaces feel like polished mahogany and brushed brass
4. Cinematic atmosphere — the UI tells a story of high-stakes elegance

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1A1A2E) as the casino void, warm gold (#D4AF37) as wealth's promise, crimson (#8B0000) for danger/red numbers, emerald (#006400) for felt/zero, antique ivory (#F5F5DC) for readability.

**Layout Paradigm:** The roulette board dominates as a central theater stage. Controls slide from edges like curtain wings. Wheel occupies a dedicated "stage" above the board. No scrolling during play.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold pinstripe borders framing every panel like a vintage casino table
- Subtle diamond-pattern texture overlay on dark surfaces
- Numbers rendered with serif typeface reminiscent of engraved chips

**Interaction Philosophy:** Every tap feels consequential. Bet placement triggers subtle "chip drop" feel. Keypad slides up like a dealer's tray. Confirmations use dramatic fade-to-black overlay.

**Animation:** Slow, deliberate transitions. Wheel spins with realistic deceleration physics. Gold shimmer on winning numbers. Panel transitions use smooth slide with parallax depth.

**Typography System:** Display: Playfair Display. Body: Roboto Slab. Numbers: Oswald.

## Idea 2: "Neon Vegas" — Retro-Futurism Meets Strip Energy

**Design Movement:** Synthwave / Retro-Futurism — electric energy of the Las Vegas Strip at midnight.

**Core Principles:**
1. Electric energy — interface pulses with neon glows and dynamic lighting
2. Bold maximalism — big numbers, vivid colors, unapologetic visual impact
3. Immersive atmosphere — user feels transported to a casino floor
4. Playful confidence — fun and exciting, not stuffy

**Color Philosophy:** Electric cyan (#00F5FF) for primary actions. Hot magenta (#FF1493) for alerts. Deep midnight blue (#0D0221) as backdrop. Neon green (#39FF14) for wins. Warm amber (#FFB800) for wheel/gold accents.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed immersive. Board at bottom two-thirds like real table perspective. Wheel floats above with glowing halo. Side panels are glass-morphism overlays. Board numbers glow when tapped.

**Signature Elements:**
- Neon glow outlines on interactive elements that pulse gently
- Subtle scanline overlay across the UI
- Chip amounts in retro LED segment font

**Interaction Philosophy:** Fast, responsive, electric. Tapping creates neon flash. Keypad appears with quick zoom-in. Wheel spin triggers light show.

**Animation:** Fast entrance with spring physics. Wheel has motion blur. Winning numbers explode with particles. Neon elements flicker like real signs.

**Typography System:** Display: Orbitron. Body: Space Grotesk. Numbers: Share Tech Mono.

## Idea 3: "The Felt" — Authentic Casino Table Experience

**Design Movement:** Skeuomorphic Realism — faithful digital recreation of an actual casino roulette table.

**Core Principles:**
1. Authentic materials — green felt texture, polished wood rail, chrome accents
2. Spatial realism — layout mimics actual physical table perspective
3. Functional clarity — every element serves the game, no decorative excess
4. Professional atmosphere — feels like a real casino, not a game app

**Color Philosophy:** Casino green (#35654D) as dominant felt surface. Rich mahogany (#4E2728) for wheel housing/rail borders. Chrome silver (#C0C0C0) for metallic accents. Classic red (#E31B23) and black (#1B1B1B) for number grid. Cream (#FFFDD0) for labels.

**Layout Paradigm:** Bird's-eye table view. Board is exact replica of real roulette table felt — same proportions, same arrangement. Wheel at head of table (top of screen). Chip rack and controls at bottom edge. History as subtle ticker along one edge.

**Signature Elements:**
- Realistic green felt texture with visible fiber grain
- Board numbers use exact same typography and color blocks as real casino table
- Polished wood rail border around entire playing surface

**Interaction Philosophy:** Mimic real casino actions. Tapping "places a chip" visually. Keypad styled like chip denomination selector. Double-tap to remove bet. Long-press for bet details.

**Animation:** Physics-based realism. Wheel spins with accurate angular momentum and deceleration. Ball bounces realistically. Chips stack with 3D perspective. Minimal decorative animation.

**Typography System:** Display: Bebas Neue. Body: Source Sans 3. Numbers: DM Sans Bold.
